## Lately Bass

Version 2024-03-21

Simulation of the famous TX81Z patch, Lately Bass. Made by Yingchun Soul (迎春心情) with Dexed and sent to FreePats on June 2023. This version has been adapted for FreePats to support SFZ and SF2 formats and is available at the URL:
http://freepats.zenvoid.org/Synthesizer/synth-bass.html
